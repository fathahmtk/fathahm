About My Professional Journey

With over 6 years of progressive experience, I am an accomplished Accountant and Financial Analyst specializing in the dynamic F&B, Medical, and Trading sectors here in Qatar. My experience includes working with a variety of businesses, including small-scale operations like caterias and groceries.

My career has been focused on providing robust financial management and strategic insights to businesses, ensuring both financial health and strict compliance with local regulations.

During my tenure at CAN International in Doha (Mar 2019 - Mar 2025), I managed the full accounting cycle, oversaw WPS payroll processing including SIF file preparation, and prepared comprehensive financial reports for internal management and audits. I also directed trade finance activities involving Tender Bonds, Letters of Guarantee (LGs), and Performance Guarantees through local banks, maintained fixed asset registers, and actively worked to improve cash flow through effective collection strategies.

Prior to this, I gained foundational experience as an Assistant Accountant at Huda Furni Mall in Kerala, India (Jan 2018 - Jan 2019), where I handled daily entries, managed vendor and customer accounts, supported month-end closings, and assisted with tax filing.

I am currently enhancing my qualifications by pursuing the **CMA USA** certification with the Institute of Management Accountants (IMA) since June 2024. I also hold a **VIBES Professional Course** certification from Sree Sankaracharya Computer Centre (Jan 2017 - Jan 2018) and a **B. Com** degree from the University of Calicut (Jul 2013 - Dec 2016).

My dedication and performance were recognized with the **Emerging Performer Award** at CAN International in 2021.

My core competencies include Full-Cycle Accounting, Financial Reporting & Auditing, Accounts Payable & Receivable, WPS Payroll Management, Bank Reconciliations, Budgeting & Forecasting, Trade Finance, Asset & Inventory Management, ERP Software & Microsoft Excel proficiency, Cash Flow Management, Documentation & Administrative Support, and Compliance & Internal Controls.
